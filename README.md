# B3Pdb: Blood-Brain Barrier-Penetrating Peptides Archive

Welcome to the official repository for B3Pdb, a comprehensive knowledgebase of blood-brain barrier (BBB)-penetrating peptides.This resource provides well-annotated experimental information to support the rational design of therapeutic peptides for central nervous system (CNS) disorders.

**Web Server:** [https://webs.iiitd.edu.in/raghava/b3pdb/](https://webs.iiitd.edu.in/raghava/b3pdb/)

## About the Database

B3Pdb addresses the challenge of delivering therapeutics across the BBB, which prevents nearly 98% of small molecule drugs and 100% of large molecules from reaching the brain.While existing resources like Brainpeps provided limited data, B3Pdb offers a unified platform with detailed experimental and therapeutic efficacy information.

The database integrates information from:
**Literature:** Manually curated research articles from PubMed and Patents.
* **Experimental Data:** In-depth analysis of *in vitro* and *in vivo* studies, including clinical trial status.

## Key Features

### Massive Dataset
**1225 total entries** (nearly double the previous standard) 
**465 unique peptides** 
**1107 native linear peptides** and **26 cyclic peptides** 

### Extensive Coverage
**35 model organisms** for *in vivo* activities 
**21 cancer cell lines** for *in vitro* activities 
**Diverse delivery modes:** Intravenous, in situ brain perfusion, intracarotid, and others 

### Rich Annotations
Each entry is cataloged in **29 fields**, including:
* Sequence, length, and D/L-conformation 
* Chemical modifications (110 C-terminal and 115 N-terminal records)
* Cargo type (Proteins, DNA, RNA, nanoparticles, small molecules) 
* Subcellular localization and uptake efficiency 

## Overview

B3Pdb provides experimentally validated data along with:
* **Transport Mechanisms:** Details on how peptides cross the BBB.
* **Therapeutic Efficacy:** Results from pre-clinical and clinical studies.
* **Structural Data:** SMILES representations for molecular analysis.
* **Cross-references:** Links to original research and patent information.

## Applications
* **Peptide Design:** Tools for designing and modifying peptides to improve BBB permeability.
* **Drug Delivery:** Rational selection of shuttle vectors for CNS-targeted cargo.
* **Clinical Research:** Guiding routes of administration to maximize benefits and minimize side effects.

## Limitations
* **Structural Data:** Current version lacks detailed 3D structural information beyond SMILES.
* **Clinical Trials:** Comprehensive clinical trial data is still being integrated as it becomes available.
